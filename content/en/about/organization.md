## Overview of Governance

The governance of Music Game Academy is designed as a **two-tier structure** consisting of the **charter level** and the **rules level**.

- **Charter level** (basis: Academy Charter): Establishes the position of Academy President
- **Rules level** (basis: Internal Academy Rules to be enacted separately): Establishes Vice President, Term-Limited Special Director, Director, and Instructor positions

Only the Academy President is defined directly in the Charter. Other positions await the enactment of internal rules.
